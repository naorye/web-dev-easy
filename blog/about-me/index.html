About Me

My name is Naor Yehudaey (naorye) and I live in the holy city Jerusalem of Israel.
I am 29 years old, married to my lovely Ilana and we have a female cat named Mutzi.

Since I remember myself I have always had a great passion developing new apps with thrilling and innovative technologies.
My first big experience was at college, in my seminarion project. Those days J2ME was the leading technology for mobile, and I had created an application that allows sending and receiving emails via mobile phone. This might sound very simple nowadays but in the middle of 2003, a lot before Android and iPhone were created, when the leading mobile in the israeli market was Nokia 3510 it was quite innovative.

Later I understood that mobile apps development is about to change among with the desktop apps development. More and more apps moved to the cloud and many different development technologies have arisen. The new invention of ajax and its use in GMail (2004) was a great jump in the web development history that made the JavaScript live again.
Ever since, I dedicate my spare time to learn and developing new web apps, implementing new ideas and learning the new fascinating possibilities of the web.
